About EU Regulation 1182/71

This calculator implements the rules for calculating periods according to EU Regulation 1182/71, which includes:

  • Periods expressed in hours, days, weeks, months, or years
  • Working days calculation (excluding weekends and holidays)
  • Special rules for month-end calculations
  • Rules for periods starting from an event or action
