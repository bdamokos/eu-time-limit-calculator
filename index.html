EU Regulation 1182/71 Date Calculator

About This Calculator

This calculator implements the rules for calculating periods according to EU Regulation 1182/71, specifically Article 3. The calculator handles:

  • Article 3(1): Excluding the day/hour of the event
  • Article 3(2): Calculating periods in hours, days, weeks, months, or years
  • Article 3(3): Including holidays and weekends by default
  • Article 3(4): Extending to the next working day if the period ends on a holiday or weekend
